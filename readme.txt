== Installation ==

= From your WordPress dashboard =

1. Visit Plugins > Add New
2. Search for AvgBot
3. Activate AvgBot from your Plugins page. (You will be greeted with a Welcome page.)
4. Visit avgbot.com and register an account. Add your site url and logo link for getting your FREE token code.
5. Get your [Free TOKEN code](https://avgbot.com/) and copy it.
6. Visit AvgBot > General Settings and insert your token code and player url to settings fields.
7. If your theme is using custom meta labels visit AvgBot > Meta Settings and register your custom label infos for your theme. If not, go to step 7.
8. Select that what you wanna import! And enjoy!

= From WordPress.org =

1. Download AvgBot.
2. Upload the avgbot directory to your /wp-content/plugins/ directory, using your favorite method (ftp, sftp, scp, etc...)
3. Activate AvgBot from your Plugins page. (You will be greeted with a Welcome page.)
4. Visit avgbot.com and register an account. Add your site url and logo link for getting your FREE token code.
5. Get your [Free TOKEN code](https://avgbot.com/) and copy it.
6. Visit AvgBot > General Settings and insert your token code and player url to settings fields.
7. If your theme is using custom meta labels visit AvgBot > Meta Settings and register your custom label infos for your theme. If not, go to step 7.
8. Select that what you wanna import! And enjoy!
